Ohio Lottery Project

Who wants to be an instant millionaire?

One ticket can change your fortunes from the proverbial rags to riches. In 2013, the Ohio lottery commission paid out $245M in cash prizes to the residents of Cuyahoga County. The cash prizes paid out represents 8.4% of the $2.9B collected in lottery sales in the state of Ohio. This app was created for you to optimize your chances to become the next millionaire by synthesizing data from multiple sources to increase your probability to win.



Lottery is one of the popular gambling around United states. Every American has spent some money in order to try his/her luck to win one of the biggest prizes ever announced in USA. Ohioan people usually play lottery from time to time and they are trying their luck in hope to win the American dream. This project is analyzing the average lottery tickets online and cash sales in Cuyahoga County for 2013 along with the total income salary per zip code for the same year.
